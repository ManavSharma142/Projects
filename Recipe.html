Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • MB Fit Oats(40gm)
  • Milk(120mL)
  • Chia Seeds(5gm)
  • Cocoa Powder(1/2tbsp)
  • Honey(1tbsp)

Receipe

  1. Add chia seeds into the oats in a bowl
  2. Add Cocoa Powder and mix well
  3. Add milk, honey into the mixture
  4. Stir, Store Overnight and enjoy in morning

Oats Milkshake

Ingredients

  • MB Fit Oats(40gm)
  • Milk(250mL)
  • Peanut butter(2tbsp)
  • Chia seeds(5gm)
  • Honey(1tbsp)

Receipe

  1. Add chia seeds into the oats in a jar
  2. Add Peanut butter
  3. Add milk, honey into the jar
  4. Blend, pour, enjoy
